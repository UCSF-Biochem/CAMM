Center for Advanced Multiphoton Microscopy

The Center for Advanced Multiphoton Microscopy (CAMM) was established in 2016 to give the UCSF community access to cutting-edge multiphoton instruments for high-speed live imaging. CAMM is a partnership between UCSF and 3i (Intelligent Imaging Innovations), which specializes in advanced multi-dimensional microscopy platforms for live imaging. The center’s first microscope is a VIVOTM 2-Photon system with the option of either dual galvanometer or resonant scanning.
